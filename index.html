Bar Roulette.

Go bar hopping with the help of Uber, Yelp and Foursquare.

Confirm your location, then confirm the neighborhood you'd like to go to.

Using Yelp or Foursquare, your choice, we find a top rated bar and keep it a secret.

We send an Uber to pick you up and deliver you to the chosen bar.

Once you've arrived we tell you the name of the bar and you have a drink...or two.
